About Me

Hi, thanks for stoping by! I am currently a Sr. Solutions Analyst at POSSIBLE in Cincinnati, OH but I am always interested in hearing about new opportunities to do exciting digital work. I am passionate about creating highly-interactive web experiences with the newest technology. Please check out my resume and digital portfolio while you are here!

Resume

My passion is for delivering highly-interactive mobile and desktop websites. In the past 7 years of my digital experience, I have developed and worked with teams to launch sites on a variety of platforms (Drupal 8, Wordpress, Java, .NET) while leveraging a variety of development tools including VM's, Grunt, Gulp, Sass, Git, Subversion, Handlebars, jQuery, Jira, Exact Target and more.

In my role at POSSIBLE, on of the largest digital agencies in the U.S., I provide technical solutions to our creative teams, gather requirements, and lead development efforts on projects. Projects I launched included:

  • ReadySetEat.com
  • ConagraBrands.com
  • ConagraNutrition.com
  • DaisyBrand.com
  • WickedKitchen.com
  • CurateSnacks.com
  • and many other Conagra brand site updates.

While an E-Commerce administrator in marketing at a large, publicly-held company, I have lead and successfully championed the adoption of new technologies including wordpress (web cms software), yammer (collaboration software), highcharts (javascript charting software), and web-based marketing tools to make The Andersons, Inc. more agile, more aggressive, and cost effective in our web strategy. In my marketing role, I've shot and produced over 1 thousand web videos, and manage a host of social media and analytics for The Andersons, Inc.

Professionally, I hope to continue to refine my development skills and put them to work managing and/or developing large-scale projects.
